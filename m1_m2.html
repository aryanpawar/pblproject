Enginnering Mathematics I

Syllabus :(Units)

  1. Differential Calculus:
  2. Fourier Series
  3. Partial Differentiation
  4. Applications of Partial Differentiation
  5. Linear Algebra-Matrices, System of Linear Equations
  6. Linear Algebra-Eigen Values and Eigen Vectors, Diagonaliztion

Resources & Books :

  • Higher Engineering Mathematics by B. V. Ramana (Tata McGraw Hill)
  • Higher Engineering Mathematics by B. S. Grewal (Khanna Publication, Delhi)
  • Advanced Engineering Mathematics by Erwin Kreyszig (Wiley Eastern Ltd.)
  • Advanced Engineering Mathematics by M. D. Greenberg (Pearson Education)
  • Advanced Engineering Mathematics by Peter V. O’Neil (Thomson Learning)
  • Thomas’ Calculus by George B. Thomas, (Addison-Wesley, Pearson)
  • Applied Mathematics (Vol. I & Vol. II) by P.N.Wartikar and J.N.Wartikar Vidyarthi Griha Prakashan, Pune.
  • Linear Algebra –An Introduction, Ron Larson, David C. Falvo (Cenage Learning, Indian edition)

Other Information :

To study engineering mathematics you need to know the basic formulae and laws of indices,trigonometric equations, differentiation, integration,etc. This is same for M-I & M-II.
