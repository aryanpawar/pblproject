BEE : Basic Eletrical Engineering

Syllabus :(Units)

  1. Electromagnetism
  2. Electrostatics & AC Fundamentals
  3. Single Phase AC Circuits
  4. Polyphase AC circuits & Single Phase Transformers
  5. DC Circuits
  6. Work, Power, Energy and Batteries

Resources & Books :

  • V.D. Toro, Principles of Electrical Engineering, Prentice Hall India, 1989
  • D. P. Kothari, I.J. Nagrath, Theory and Problems of Basic Electrical Engineering, PHI Publication
  • V.K. Mehta, RohitMehata Basic Electrical Engineering, S Chand Publications
  • B.L. Theraja, A text book on electrical technology Vol-I
  • H Cotton, Electrical technology, CBS Publication
  • L. S. Bobrow, ―Fundamentals of Electrical Engineering‖, Oxford University Press, 2011
  • E. Hughes, ―Electrical and Electronics Technology‖, Pearson, 2010.
  • D. C. Kulshreshtha, ―Basic Electrical Engineering‖, McGraw Hill, 2009

Other Information :

To study an electrical engineering degree, you will need a strong background in mathematics and science subjects (especially physics). However, don’t be deterred; many courses will start with some foundation modules designed to ensure this background knowledge is up to the required standard.
